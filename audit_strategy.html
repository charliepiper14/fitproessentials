Executive Summary

Critical Issues Requiring Immediate Action

  • Attribution Gap: Meta reports 31 purchases against 90 confirmed sales. Approximately 66 percent of revenue is not being attributed to paid campaigns.
  • Audience Saturation: Warm campaign frequency has reached 5.54. Industry best practice recommends maintaining frequency below 3.0 to prevent creative fatigue.
  • Algorithm Disruption: Multiple audience modifications between January 3 and 6 reset learning phase, causing cost per acquisition to increase from $8.74 to $18.35.
  • Content Club Performance: $58.97 invested with zero purchases. The recurring payment model presents a significant conversion barrier.
  • Campaign Status: 6-Week Challenge campaigns paused on January 7 following consistent performance decline.

Immediate Opportunities

  • Order Value Performance: Actual average order value of $52.60 against $27 front-end price demonstrates strong upsell funnel execution.
  • Proven Creative: HeyGen AI video achieving $14.01 cost per acquisition with Meta Above Average conversion ranking.
  • Funnel Conversion Rates: Order bump converting at 16.7 percent, $97 upsell at 20 percent, and $1 Coach Engine trial at 3.3 percent.
  • Sugar Detox Launch Ready: New $12 lead magnet is live and awaiting traffic allocation.
  • Seasonal Timing: January represents peak acquisition period for fitness coaching products.

Strategic Considerations

  • Customer Composition: 73 percent of sales originated from returning customers. Cold traffic acquisition requires improvement.
  • Coach Engine Creative: Current creative direction flagged as too dark with messaging that over-promises. Complete redesign required.
  • Content Club Positioning: Recurring subscription model facing resistance from AI competition and subscription fatigue.

Performance Escalation Protocol
